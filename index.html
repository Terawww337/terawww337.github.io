About

I’m Terawww — a solo indie developer. I focus on psychological horror and meta design: systems that feel alive, interfaces that lie, and stories told through interaction.

My current flagship project is WriteNor — a meta-horror coding sim built around terminal commands, corrupted logic, and an AI entity called NORI.

> DEV_PROFILE: Terawww
> DOMAIN: Psycho-horror / Meta / Diegetic UI
> ACTIVE_PROJECT: WriteNor (Steam + Web)

Focus

Atmosphere Engineering

Sound pressure, visual noise, pacing control, and subtle “wrongness” without cheap jumpscares.

System Horror

Mechanics that behave like a hostile OS: logs, permissions, corrupted states, and unreliable feedback.

Diegetic Interfaces

UI inside the world: screens, terminals, overlays, and interaction that feels physical.

Philosophy

Horror works best when it’s logical. Not explained — but consistent. The player should feel the rules, then realize the rules can be rewritten.

Meta isn’t “breaking the 4th wall” for jokes. It’s turning the interface into a threat: menus, saves, prompts, and “helpful” systems that become unreliable.

> DESIGN_NOTE: “Make the player doubt the UI, not the monster.”
> PRIORITY: Tension > Lore dumps > Explainers
